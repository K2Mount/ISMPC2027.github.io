About ISMPC 2027

The 9th International Symposium on Monolayer-Protected Clusters will convene the global community in Singapore for scientific exchange at the frontier of atomically precise nanomaterials.

About ISMPC

ISMPC is a focused international forum dedicated to the chemistry, physics, theory, characterization, and applications of monolayer-protected clusters. The symposium encourages discussion across disciplines and career stages.

The 9th Edition

Hosted in Singapore at NUS University Town, ISMPC 2027 will highlight new discoveries, emerging instrumentation, and collaborative directions for the next generation of cluster science.

Scope

Symposium Topics

The program will span fundamental methods, structures, properties, and applications.

  • Synthesis of monolayer-protected clusters
  • Atomic precision and structure
  • Spectroscopy and theory
  • Self-assembly and interfaces
  • Catalysis and energy
  • Bio-applications and sensing
  • Emerging methods and instrumentation
