About ISMPC2027

The 9th International Symposium on Monolayer-Protected Clusters will bring the international cluster science community to Singapore from 1 to 4 August 2027.

About the ISMPC Series

ISMPC is a long-standing international symposium series dedicated to advances in atomically precise metal nanoclusters and related systems. Since the inaugural meeting in Finland in 2008, the symposium has brought together researchers worldwide to discuss recent developments in cluster synthesis, structure, properties, and emerging applications.

ISMPC2027 in Singapore

ISMPC2027 will bring the international cluster science community to Singapore from 1 to 4 August 2027. Hosted in the environment of NUS University Town, the symposium will provide a focused setting for scientific exchange, collaboration, and discussion of emerging directions in monolayer-protected clusters and atomically precise nanomaterials.
