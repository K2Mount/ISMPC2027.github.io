About ISMPC 2027

The 9th International Symposium on Monolayer-Protected Clusters (ISMPC2027) will be held in Singapore from 1 to 4 August 2027. As a long-standing international symposium series, ISMPC brings together researchers working on atomically precise metal nanoclusters, ligand-protected nanomaterials, and related systems to discuss recent advances in synthesis, structure, properties, and emerging applications.

Venue

Located within the National University of Singapore, University Town offers a lively and well-connected campus setting for ISMPC2027. With modern academic facilities, green open spaces, cafés, and informal gathering areas, UTown provides an ideal environment for scientific exchange, networking, and community building throughout the symposium.
