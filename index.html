About ISMPC2027

The 9th International Symposium on Monolayer-Protected Clusters (ISMPC2027) will be held in Singapore from 1 to 4 August 2027. As a long-standing international symposium series, ISMPC brings together researchers working on atomically precise metal nanoclusters, ligand-protected nanomaterials, and related systems to discuss recent advances in synthesis, structure, properties, and emerging applications.

Cluster Science

Researchers worldwide gathering to exchange ideas and build collaborations.

Emerging Applications

New directions in catalysis, energy, biointerfaces, sensing, and materials science.

Venue

NUS University Town

ISMPC2027 will be hosted in NUS University Town, a lively academic district with lecture venues, open plazas, green courtyards, residential colleges, cafes, and dining spaces within a short walk.

The main scientific sessions will use The Ngee Ann Kongsi Auditorium in the Education Resource Centre, with UTown serving as the conference base for lectures, poster interaction, informal meetings, and campus hospitality.
