About ISMPC 2027

ISMPC is the premier international forum for advancing the science of monolayer-protected clusters and atomically precise nanomaterials. The symposium brings together leading researchers from chemistry, physics, materials science, and related disciplines to share discoveries, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations that shape the future of nanoscale science.
