About ISMPC 2027

The 9th International Symposium on Monolayer-Protected Clusters (ISMPC 2027) will be held in Singapore from 1 to 4 August 2027. As a long-standing international symposium series, ISMPC brings together researchers working on atomically precise metal nanoclusters, ligand-protected nanomaterials, and related systems to discuss recent advances in synthesis, structure, properties, and emerging applications.

Venue

The Ngee Ann Kongsi Auditorium, NUS University Town

ISMPC 2027 will be held at The Ngee Ann Kongsi Auditorium, located on Level 3 of the Education Resource Centre (ERC) in NUS University Town. The 200-seat auditorium offers a modern setting for plenary sessions, keynote lectures, panel discussions, and scientific exchange.

UTown combines modern academic facilities, green open spaces, and nearby dining options, providing a convenient and vibrant environment for conference participants.
