Before Departure

Most non-Singapore passport holders need at least six months' passport validity. Keep proof of onward travel and sufficient funds available if requested.

Visa Applications

If a visa is required, ICA directs visitors to apply online through a local contact or via authorised visa agents / Singapore Overseas Missions.

SG Arrival Card

Submit within three days before arrival. The official SGAC service is free; avoid unofficial paid submission sites.

Transport to NUS

Taxi / Private Hire

Simplest with luggage. Allow roughly 25-40 minutes from Changi Airport, depending on traffic.

  • Destination: Education Resource Centre, NUS University Town
  • Ride apps and taxis are widely available at Changi Airport
  • Best for late arrivals or hotel check-in with baggage

MRT + Campus Transfer

Take the East-West Line from Changi Airport via Tanah Merah, transfer at Buona Vista to the Circle Line, then continue toward Kent Ridge or one-north.

  • Kent Ridge MRT: convenient for NUS and Citadines Science Park
  • one-north MRT: convenient for lyf and Rochester hotels
  • Use contactless bank cards, mobile wallets, or stored-value cards

Getting Around Singapore

Singapore has an integrated network of trains, buses, taxis, and private-hire cars. Rail services generally run from early morning to around midnight.

  • Useful apps: Google Maps, MyTransport.SG, Grab, CDG Zig
  • Single-trip rail tickets have been phased out
  • Plan extra time for campus transfers in August weather

Weather & Packing

Singapore is tropical year-round. August is warm, humid, and rain showers can arrive quickly.

25.3°CMean daily minimum in August

Recommended

  • Light clothing plus a layer for air-conditioned rooms
  • Umbrella or compact rain jacket
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Reusable water bottle, sunscreen, and Type G adapter
