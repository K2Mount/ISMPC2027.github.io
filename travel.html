Entry & Visa

Check official Singapore government sources before booking. Visa requirements depend on passport, nationality, travel document, and transit status.

Official Entry Requirements Check Visa List

Before Departure

Passport validity of at least six months is required for most non-Singapore passport holders. Keep proof of onward travel and sufficient funds available if requested at immigration.

Visa Applications

If a visa is required, ICA indicates applications can be submitted online through a local contact or through authorised visa agents / Singapore Overseas Missions.

SG Arrival Card

Submit within three days before arrival. The official SGAC service is free; avoid unofficial paid submission sites.

Transport to NUS

Taxi / Private Hire

Simplest with luggage. Allow roughly 25-40 minutes from Changi Airport, depending on traffic.

  • Destination: Education Resource Centre, NUS University Town
  • Ride apps and taxis are widely available at Changi Airport
  • Best option for late arrivals or hotel check-in with baggage

MRT + Campus Transfer

For public transport, take the East-West Line from Changi Airport via Tanah Merah, transfer at Buona Vista to the Circle Line, and continue toward Kent Ridge.

  • Nearest MRT for Citadines / NUS area: Kent Ridge (CC24)
  • Nearest MRT for lyf / one-north hotels: one-north (CC23)
  • Use a contactless bank card, mobile wallet, or stored-value card for fares

Getting Around Singapore

LTA notes Singapore has an extensive integrated network of trains, buses, taxis, and private-hire cars. Rail operating hours are generally from about 5:30 am to around midnight.

  • Useful apps: Google Maps, MyTransport.SG, Grab, CDG Zig
  • Public transport payment: contactless cards, mobile wallets, or stored-value cards
  • Single-trip rail tickets have been phased out

Weather & Packing

Singapore is tropical year-round. The Meteorological Service Singapore notes high humidity, abundant rainfall, and small month-to-month temperature variation. August falls within the Southwest Monsoon season.

31.4°CMean daily maximum in August
25.3°CMean daily minimum in August
14Mean August rain days
80.7%Mean August relative humidity

Recommended

  • Light breathable clothing plus a layer for air-conditioned lecture rooms
  • Umbrella or compact rain jacket for short showers
  • Comfortable walking shoes for campus and Marina Bay walks
  • Reusable water bottle, sunscreen, and travel adapter, Type G plug
