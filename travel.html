Transport to NUS

Taxi / Private Hire

Simplest with luggage. Allow roughly 25-40 minutes from Changi Airport, depending on traffic.

  • Destination: Education Resource Centre, NUS University Town
  • Ride apps and taxis are widely available at Changi Airport
  • Best for late arrivals or hotel check-in with baggage

MRT + Campus Transfer

Take the East-West Line from Changi Airport via Tanah Merah, transfer at Buona Vista to the Circle Line, then continue to Kent Ridge MRT for campus transfer to NUS.

  • Kent Ridge MRT: main transfer point for NUS campus access
  • UTown stop: closest campus shuttle stop for the ISMPC2027 venue
  • Use contactless bank cards, mobile wallets, or stored-value cards

Getting Around Singapore

Singapore has an integrated network of trains, buses, taxis, and private-hire cars. Rail services generally run from early morning to around midnight.

  • Useful apps: Google Maps, MyTransport.SG, Grab, CDG Zig
  • Single-trip rail tickets have been phased out
  • Plan extra time for campus transfers in August weather

Campus transfer guide

After arriving at NUS, participants can use the NUS Internal Shuttle Bus network for campus transfers. The main symposium venue is located at NUS University Town; participants should use the UTown stop for the Education Resource Centre and The Ngee Ann Kongsi Auditorium.

From Kent Ridge MRTKent Ridge MRT is a key transfer point for participants entering NUS by public transport. Follow campus signs and use the appropriate NUS internal shuttle bus or local bus connection toward UTown.
The Ridge accommodationThe Ridge is located near the AS5 stop. Participants staying there should check the shuttle route map and allow extra transfer time during peak campus hours.

Weather & Packing

Singapore is tropical year-round. August is warm, humid, and rain showers can arrive quickly.
