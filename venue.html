Venue

NUS University Town

ISMPC 2027 will be hosted at NUS University Town, National University of Singapore, a vibrant academic setting for international scientific exchange.

Venue Description

NUS University Town brings together modern lecture facilities, residential colleges, research spaces, and public gathering areas in a walkable campus environment. It is well connected to central Singapore and Changi Airport.
