Conference Environment

NUS University Town (UTown)

ISMPC 2027 will be hosted in NUS University Town (UTown), a vibrant academic hub at the National University of Singapore. With its modern teaching and event facilities, green open spaces, and nearby dining options, UTown provides an engaging and convenient environment for scientific exchange and networking.

Within UTown, the conference activities will be centered around the Education Resource Centre (ERC), one of the district's key academic venues. The main scientific sessions will be held in The Ngee Ann Kongsi Auditorium, located on Level 3 of ERC.

The 200-seat auditorium offers a modern setting for plenary lectures, keynote presentations, and panel discussions, while the surrounding foyer and common areas support informal interactions, tea breaks, and poster-related activities.
